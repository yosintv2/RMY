What is BMI?

BMI, or Body Mass Index, is a measure that uses your height and weight to calculate whether your weight is healthy. Itâ€™s widely used by healthcare professionals to assess body fat levels and associated health risks.

How to Use the BMI Calculator?

Simply enter your age, weight (in kilograms), and height (in centimeters) in the fields provided. Click on "Calculate BMI" to see your result and classification according to international BMI standards.

Why is BMI Important?

BMI is a quick and easy screening tool to categorize individuals based on their weight in relation to their height. It helps in identifying potential health risks such as underweight, overweight, or obesity, and promotes awareness of maintaining a healthy lifestyle.
